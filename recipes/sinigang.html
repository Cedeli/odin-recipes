Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a sour soup native to the Philippines. This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient. Other proteins and seafood can also be used. Beef, shrimp, fish are commonly used to cook sinigang.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Pork
  • 1 Bunch Kang-Kong
  • 3 tbsp Fish Sauce
  • 12 Pieces String Beans
  • 2 Pieces Quarterd Tomato
  • 3 Pieces Chili
  • 1 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 8 Cups Water
  • 1 Piece Sliced Onion
  • 2 Piece Quartered Taro
  • 1 Pack Sinigang Mix
